How to Use

  1. Download the "Certificate of Enrollment" PDF file of your schedule from the SIAS portal.
  2. Drag and drop that PDF file directly into the upload section above, or click to browse for it.
  3. Your organized weekly schedule will appear as a preview.
  4. Select your favorite theme and click the "Export Schedule as Image" button to save it.

Features

Thematic Export

Export your schedule as a beautiful wallpaper that matches your chosen theme.

Modern UI

Clean and stylish design with multiple themes like Dark, Lightning, and seasonal styles.

Direct PDF Parsing

Directly processes your schedule PDF, eliminating the need for manual file conversion.
